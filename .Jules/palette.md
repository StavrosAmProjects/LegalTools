## 2025-05-15 - [The "Golden" Standard for Legal Tech]
**Learning:** In legal environments, UX "delight" is often synonymous with "absolute clarity and authority." High-contrast, serif-heavy designs (the "Onyx and Gold" aesthetic) signal professionalism and trustworthiness to legal users, which is more effective than the "playful" delight found in consumer apps.
**Action:** Use serif fonts (Playfair Display, etc.) and deep dark themes for professional legal dashboards to maintain a "Calibration Terminal" feel.

## 2025-05-15 - [Keyboard First for Power Users]
**Learning:** Legal professionals often move between many documents and agents. A "Command-K" global search pattern is not just a shortcut but a core navigation requirement for power users in this domain.
**Action:** Always implement a Cmd+K/Ctrl+K search interface as the primary navigation fallback for complex legal agent repositories.
